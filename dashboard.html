02 Cleaning & Normalization

Procrustes era align + RealMLP robust + quality filter 1,366 docs

Why clean?

Raw NBA stats drift by era (pace, 3PT rate). We z-score each season vs its own league → era-honest sigmas. Then Procrustes align archetype spaces so 1999 and 2025 compare fairly. RealMLP robust scaling handles outliers (e.g., 73-win seasons).

  • ✅ Per-100 sigmas per season — 120 feats 17 families
  • ✅ Procrustes era_procrustes_align.py — temporal drift ↓ 18%
  • ✅ RealMLP realmlp_preproc.py — robust norm + PL embeddings
  • ✅ Quality filter fast heuristic alpha/uniq/len — 1,366 filtered
  • ✅ Dedup md5 fast (Hatch) / simhash full (Alienware)
